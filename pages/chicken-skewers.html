01

Cut your salmon into cubes

Buying boneless skinless salmon will save you the work of removing the skin (which is how I like it)! Cubes are good because the spice coating gets everywhere, and the salmon cooks very evenly. No dry pieces! 100% juicy.

02

Toss it with the spices


Get all the powders in the mentioned amounts: 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, 1 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon cornstarch, 3/4 teaspoon coarse kosher salt (or 1/2 teaspoon table salt). Mix well and add some olive oil.

Every piece gets coated with flavor and texture, and a few teaspoons of olive oil will help it all hold nicely! Just how we like it!
